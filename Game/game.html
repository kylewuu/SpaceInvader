CONTROLS:
Move left and Right: A or LeftArrow and D or RightArrow
Fire: SPACE or Left Mouse Click
R: RESTART
Q: QUIT
The goal of the game is to destory the red enemeies by firing missiles at them while avoiding the red missiles. Victory is achieved if all the enemies are defeated before they reach the bottom of the screen or before they destory you.
